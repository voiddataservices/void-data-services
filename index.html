Our Data Services

Data Entry Services
We provide accurate and secure data entry from PDFs, scanned documents, handwritten forms, websites, and physical records into Excel, Word, or databases. Our process includes validation, formatting, and quality checks to ensure reliable and business-ready data.
PDF to Excel / Word Conversion
We convert PDF files into fully editable Excel and Word formats while preserving layout, tables, and structure. Scanned PDFs are processed using OCR when required to deliver searchable and editable business documents.
Image to Text Data Conversion
We extract text and structured data from images, scanned photos, and screenshots into clean, editable formats. Ideal for receipts, ID records, printed forms, and image-based business documents.
Data Cleaning & Formatting
We clean messy datasets by removing duplicates, fixing errors, standardizing formats, correcting spelling, and organizing columns. This ensures high-quality, analysis-ready, and professional business data.
Excel Data Management
We manage large Excel files by organizing sheets, applying formulas, sorting, filtering, and structuring data for easy tracking, reporting, and business decision-making.
Web Research & Data Collection
We research websites and online sources to collect targeted business data such as company details, contacts, emails, pricing, and market information. Delivered in clean Excel or CSV formats.
